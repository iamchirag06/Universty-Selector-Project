About KLN College of Engineering

KLN College of Engineering (KLNCE) is a premier educational institution located in Pottapalayam, near Madurai. Established in 1994, KLNCE is affiliated to Anna University and is approved by AICTE. The college is managed by the KLN Trust and is named after philanthropist and industrialist K.L.N. Krishnan.

KLNCE is known for its commitment to academic excellence, providing state-of-the-art infrastructure, and fostering holistic student development. With dedicated faculty and a vibrant campus culture, KLNCE prepares students to excel in their academic and professional careers.

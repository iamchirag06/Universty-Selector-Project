About Anna University of Technology, Madurai

Anna University of Technology, Madurai was established as a technical university by the Government of Tamil Nadu to foster higher education and research in the southern region of the state. Located in Keelakuilkudi, it served as a hub for technical education for many affiliated engineering colleges.

The university offered undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering and technology. In 2012, the university was merged back into Anna University, Chennai as part of a state government reform, but its legacy and affiliated institutions continue to function under Anna University’s umbrella.
